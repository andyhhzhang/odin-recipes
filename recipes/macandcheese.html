Mac and Cheese

Description

Follow this recipe to make the best Thanksgiving party mac n cheese

Ingredients

  • 1 lb dried pasta
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups of whole milk
  • 2 1/2 cupers half and half
  • 4 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 cups shredded Gruyere cheese
  • 1/2 Tbsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp. paprika

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta, then drain
  2. Make the cheese sauce by combining butter and flour
  3. Whisk in the milk products
  4. Cook the sauce until it's nice and thick
  5. Added in shredded cheeses and stir well
  6. Combine cheese sauce with cooked pasta
  7. Transfer half of the mac and cheese to a baking dish and sprinkle with more shredded cheese
  8. Top with the rest of the mac and cheese and top with more shredded cheese
  9. Bake and prepare to thoroughly enjoy!
