Milkshake

Description

Follow this recipe to make the perfect milkshake for a hot summer day

Ingredients

  • Whipped cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 4 scoops of any ice cream
  • 1 cherry
  • Sprinkles

Steps

  1. Add the ice cream to the blender
  2. Add the milk to the blender
  3. Blend on low until smooth
  4. Pour into a cup and garnish with whipped cream
  5. Put some sprinkles on top
  6. Top it off with a cherry
